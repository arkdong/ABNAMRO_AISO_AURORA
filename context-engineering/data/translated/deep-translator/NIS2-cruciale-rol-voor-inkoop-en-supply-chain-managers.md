#NIS2: crucial role for purchasing and supply chain managers

[Knowledge article](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/actueel.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

19/05/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

More than 10,000 Dutch companies will soon have to meet the requirements of the NIS2 directive. But the consequences are not limited to this group. An estimated 50,000 suppliers, from IT partners to cleaning companies, are also affected. This makes the role of purchasing and supply chain managers more important than ever.

Every supplier in the chain can pose a potential risk. A weak link can lead to service disruptions, reputational damage or legal consequences. Purchasing and supply chain managers are therefore indispensable in identifying, discussing and managing cybersecurity risks within the entire chain. This requires sharpness, cooperation and expert knowledge. But luckily you don't have to do it alone.

## Especially for purchasing professionals: six practical videos

The cyber platform Samen Digitaal Veilig developed six practical [videos] (https://samendigitaalveilig.nl/inkoop/) especially for purchasing professionals. The videos focus on the daily practice of purchasing and supply chain managers.

This makes it clear:

- What the role of purchasing is within NIS2
- How to recognize risks with suppliers
- How to make suppliers aware of their responsibility
- How to collaborate effectively with IT and legal colleagues
- What tools are available for a smooth implementation
- Why the NIS2 Quality Mark is an accessible alternative for tough ISO processes
