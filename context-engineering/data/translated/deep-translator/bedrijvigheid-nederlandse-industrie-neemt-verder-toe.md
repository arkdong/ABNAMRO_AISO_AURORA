# Dutch industry activity continues to increase

[Analysis](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/analyses/index.html)

[Industry](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/industrie/index.html)

01/08/2025 • 2 minutes reading

[Albert Jan Swart](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/authors/albert-jan-swart/index.html)

The Nevi Purchasing Managers Index for Dutch industry rose further in July, from 51.2 to 51.9. This seems to indicate a further increase in activity.

Both production and the number of new orders increased, as did employment and the purchase of materials. Many entrepreneurs are seeing the number of new orders increasing, both from the Netherlands and abroad. The number of new orders rose in July at the fastest pace in a year.

The increase in the Nevi Purchasing Managers Index has been continuing for a number of months now, possibly due to the recovery of the chip market. However, the index is still slightly lower than the long-term average. And whether the recovery will continue remains to be seen.

## Changes in US tariffs

The coming months will show whether European exports to the United States (US) will come under further pressure. On July 27, US President Donald Trump and European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen signed a trade agreement. On the positive side, additional American duties on European cars will be reduced from 25 to 15 percent, although the rates are now approximately six times higher than before Trump introduced these import duties in the spring. US import duties on pharmaceutical products are 15 percent. Until now, no additional tax has applied to these products. Pharmaceutical products are important export products for the Dutch industry for the American market. Chip machines and related equipment, also important for Dutch industry, may be exempt from import duties.

The US has imposed a 50 percent import tariff on steel and aluminum since the beginning of June. This tariff may be maintained, which could hit the European steel industry. In any case, it is reassuring for Dutch industry that trade tensions have not escalated further and the European Commission is not hitting back with import duties on American products. The European industry imports aircraft parts from the US, among other things.

## Read more in the industrial sector

The industry is a heavyweight in the Dutch economy. Export is an important driver for this, but at the same time creates uncertainty. There are also themes that have a positive influence on growth, such as cooperation in the chain, innovation and sustainability.
