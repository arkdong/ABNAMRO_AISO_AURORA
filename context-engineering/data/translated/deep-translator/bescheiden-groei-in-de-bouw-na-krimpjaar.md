# Modest growth in construction after a year of decline

[Podcast](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-spraak/podcast/index.html)

[Construction](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/bouw/index.html)

19/08/2025 • 1 minute read

[Leontien de Waal](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/authors/leontien-de-waal/index.html)

After a year of decline in 2024, we will see modest growth in construction again in the coming years. The sector is growing mainly thanks to housing construction, while there are all kinds of bottlenecks that slow down the implementation of projects. Consider higher construction costs, staff shortages, grid congestion and delays in permitting by municipalities. In this video, sector banker Leontien de Waal explains in 2 minutes what challenges construction faces and what you can do as an entrepreneur.
