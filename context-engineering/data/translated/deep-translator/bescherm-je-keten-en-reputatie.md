# Protect your chain and reputation

[Webinar](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-conversation/webinar/index.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

05/28/2025 • 1 minute read

ABN AMRO Editorial

In a world in which digitalization has become indispensable, cyber risks are increasing rapidly. Suppliers are important partners, but also a risk factor for continuity and customer satisfaction. During this webinar, experts discuss how you can strengthen the digital resilience of your retail company.

Under the leadership of Henk Hofstede, sector banker at ABN AMRO, we zoom in on cybersecurity challenges and discuss effective solutions. Joining him at the table are cybersecurity expert at ABN AMRO [Richard Verbrugge](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/expert-academy/security-awareness-onderwijs.html), Ron Vermeulen of Samen Digitaal Veilig, Vincent Romviel of Thuiswinkel.org and Maarten Roerink of MMOX.

## Topics during the webinar

- The importance of supply chain security in retail and how to protect your chain against cyber threats.
- Methods to minimize reputational damage in the event of a cyber attack.
- Practical security tips that you can use tomorrow.
