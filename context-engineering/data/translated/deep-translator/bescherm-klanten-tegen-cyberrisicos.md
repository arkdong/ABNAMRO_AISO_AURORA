# Protect customers against cyber risks

[Webinar](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-conversation/webinar/index.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

14/05/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

The digital transformation is rapidly changing the food and manufacturing industry, increasing cyber risks. Suppliers and subcontractors are not only important partners, but also a risk factor. Your organization can also pose a risk to your customers. Experts discuss the increase in cyber risks during a webinar.

Under the leadership of David Kemps (Sector Banker at ABN AMRO), Richard Verbrugge, cybersecurity expert at ABN AMRO, Daan Hoogendijk (program director of Samen Digitaal Veilig) and Wim Kannegieter (Director of the Dutch Bakery Association) discuss the cyber threats and opportunities within the food and (manufacturing) industry.

In addition, Bart van Es, IT Manager at Trymax Semiconductor B.V. speaking. Trymax Semiconductor B.V. has been working on the security of the organization for some time.

## Topics during the webinar

- The role of supply chain security in the food and (manufacturing) industry and how you can protect the chain against cyber threats, with a focus on compliance with NIS2 requirements.
- Practical steps and strategies to strengthen your digital security in line with the new guideline.
