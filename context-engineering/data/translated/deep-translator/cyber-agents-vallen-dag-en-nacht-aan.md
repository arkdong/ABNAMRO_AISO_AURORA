## Without human guidance

Cyber Agents are autonomous programs that scan networks for weaknesses. They attack without human guidance. Unlike classic malware, they work as digital hunters. They immediately adjust their approach if systems defend themselves better.

## Why Cyber Agents are Dangerous

The biggest risk is their speed and scale. A human hacker can only attack one system at a time. Cyber ‚Äã‚ÄãAgents can attack thousands of systems simultaneously, day and night. They are difficult to stop and almost impossible to trace back to a perpetrator.

## Future music, but not far away

This technology is still in development, but reality is getting closer. Experts expect that entrepreneurs will soon have to deal with this threat. It is important to take steps now. Think of better collaboration, sharing information and strengthening digital infrastructure.

## Prepare the company

Cyber Agents herald a new era in cyber threats. Machines fight machines. This emphasizes the need for strong digital defense. Protect your company by investing in cybersecurity in a timely manner. Don't wait until it's too late.
