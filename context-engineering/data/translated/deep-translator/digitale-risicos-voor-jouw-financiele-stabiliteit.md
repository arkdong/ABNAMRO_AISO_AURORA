# Digital risks for your financial stability

[Knowledge article](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/actueel.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

11/17/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

Cyber hygiene is indispensable for financial stability. It is not only about technical solutions such as passwords and firewalls, but also about good governance. Anyone who does not have their digital foundation in order endangers business operations, reputation and financial health.

The European NIS2 directive makes digital security a task for the board. It's about more than IT: business continuity, risks and integrity are at stake. A cyber incident can halt production, lose customers and lead to legal claims. Good governance also means taking responsibility for digital security.

## Cybersecurity is also a financial issue

Cyber security has direct consequences for financial reporting and corporate position:

- Accountants test continuity in the annual accounts. Without demonstrable safety, comments or limitations arise in the audit report.
- Customers and partners are increasingly demanding digital security in contracts. Anyone who cannot provide proof of a safely operating organization runs the risk of losing orders. The chain clauses from NIS2 make this even more urgent.

## The basics are in order with demonstrable cyber hygiene

A good digital foundation starts with structural measures that you can also demonstrate. The NIS2 Quality Mark is a quality mark that proves that your company meets security standards. Independent control ensures trust among customers and partners.

## Protect your future

Cyber security is more than technology. It protects your data, your reputation and your financial stability. Organizations that invest in digital security not only protect their data, but also their reputation, their customers and their future.
