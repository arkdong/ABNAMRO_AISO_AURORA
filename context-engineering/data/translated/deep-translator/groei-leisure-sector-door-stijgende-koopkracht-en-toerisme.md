# Growth of the leisure sector due to increasing purchasing power and tourism

[Podcast](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-spraak/podcast/index.html)

[Leisure](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/leisure/index.html)

08/14/2025 • 1 minute read

[Gerarda Westerhuis](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/authors/gerarda-westerhuis/index.html)

The leisure sector will grow by 1.5 percent in volume in 2025 and by another 1 percent in 2026. The growth of the sector is driven by an increase in purchasing power and growth in tourism. Consumers may become more cautious in their spending due to geopolitical uncertainties. In this video, sector economist Gerarda Westerhuis explains in 2 minutes what challenges the leisure sector faces and what you can do as an entrepreneur.

## VAT increase on overnight stays

For 2026, the VAT increase on overnight stays from 9 to 21 percent will be higher than the sector. As a result, the competitive position of Dutch accommodations may deteriorate compared to those in Belgium and Germany.
