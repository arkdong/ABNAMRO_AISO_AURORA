## Adapt to economic changes and new regulations

Real Estate Sector Banker Claire van Staaij from ABN AMRO shares her vision on market developments in this podcast. The obligation of a minimum energy label C ensures more energy-efficient buildings in the office market. There is now a division in this market. Building owners must continue to invest in making their office real estate more sustainable, especially now that users are increasingly focusing on sustainability.

Real estate investors active in rental properties have been facing headwinds for several years. Various tax changes and other measures aimed at protecting tenants are hitting investors in the pockets. This has been a reason for more and more investors to sell their rental properties and offer them on the owner-occupied market. That process accelerated in 2024. As a result, a rental property is no longer the investment object it was years ago.
