#NIS2 law is expected to come into effect in spring 2026

[Webinar](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-conversation/webinar/index.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

11/08/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

After several delays, it is now almost certain: the Dutch implementation of the NIS2 directive will come into effect in the second quarter of 2026. Although the law was originally scheduled to come into effect on January 1, 2025, this proved to be unfeasible for many organizations.

## Why that new date is so likely

1. The legal text is ready - The consultation version was already published in 2023. There is political support and no substantive opposition.
2. Pressure from Europe – The Netherlands is late in implementing and risks an infringement procedure.
3. Sectors are already on the move – More than 100 industries are preparing. The demand for audits and certification is increasing.
4. Infrastructure is ready - Reporting points, standards and guidance have largely been set up, including the NIS2 Quality Mark.

## What does this mean for companies?

Anyone who is already working on getting processes, supplier management and risk management in order is not doing so too early, but right on time. Experts emphasize: doing nothing now is no longer an option.
