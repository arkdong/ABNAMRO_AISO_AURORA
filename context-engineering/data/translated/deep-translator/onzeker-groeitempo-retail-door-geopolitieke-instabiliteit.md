# Uncertain retail growth rate due to geopolitical instability

[Gerarda Westerhuis](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/authors/gerarda-westerhuis/index.html)

In 2025, we expect a slight volume growth of 1 percent for retail, partly due to a recovery in purchasing power. However, the growth rate remains uncertain due to external factors such as geopolitical instability. In this video, sector economist Gerarda Westerhuis explains in 2 minutes what challenges retail faces and what you can do as an entrepreneur.

## Growth differences between the retail sectors

Growth varies between the different branches within the retail sector. Drugstores in particular are doing well, while furniture and consumer electronics stores are struggling to grow their sales volumes and turnover.
