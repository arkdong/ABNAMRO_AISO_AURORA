# Surviving in the digital jungle

[Webinar](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-conversation/webinar/index.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

25/06/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

In the rapidly changing world of business services, it is crucial to have your cybersecurity strategy in order. It is a challenge to find your way around the new rules and requirements. How do you ensure that your organization not only survives, but also thrives in this digital jungle? During this webinar, experts discuss how you can strengthen the digital resilience of your company.

During the webinar, Han Mester, Business Services Sector Banker at ABN AMRO, and Richard Verbrugge, Cybersecurity expert at ABN AMRO, sketch a picture of what is going on in the sector in the field of cybersecurity. In addition, Maarten Roerink from MMOX, Lawyer Joe Jay de Haas at Lawfox and Bas Kroontje from Pangea sit at the table to discuss making smart choices and increasing cyber resilience.

## Topics during the webinar

1. Own management or outsourcing?: What is the best strategy for your organization? We discuss the pros and cons of keeping your IT infrastructure in-house versus outsourcing as much as possible. And how do you choose the right ICT service provider? Discover which criteria are important.
2. The role of employees: Employees are the foundation of your cybersecurity strategy. Discover how you can increase their resilience and involve them in protecting data.
3. Data protection of BYOD: Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) offers flexibility, but also entails risks. Learn how to effectively protect data in a BYOD environment.
