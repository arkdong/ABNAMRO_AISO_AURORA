# From cyber risks to solutions

[Webinar](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-conversation/webinar/index.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

05/27/2025 • 1 minute read

ABN AMRO Editorial

Digital transformation offers opportunities for logistics companies, but at the same time increases the vulnerability of the chain. One in five companies is now affected by a cyber attack. The use of AI also makes cyber attacks more dangerous. During a webinar, experts discuss the increase in cyber risks in the Transport & Logistics sector.

Under the leadership of moderator Rieneke Kok, Richard Verbrugge - cybersecurity expert at ABN AMRO - and Marijn van Schoote - managing director at FERM and CISO at Port of Rotterdam - discuss cyber threats and the impact of the NIS2 legislation.

Frank Maaskant, Warehouse Manager at Windhorst Transport, will also speak. Frank shares how the company ensures cybersecurity.

## Topics during the webinar

- The most important cybersecurity risks for logistics companies in 2025
- The impact of the NIS2 directive on the sector
- How to deal with cyber incidents and what response strategies are necessary
- Practical insights and best practices
