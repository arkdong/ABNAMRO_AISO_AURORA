# Strengthen purchasing conditions with NIS2 clauses

[Knowledge article](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/actueel.html)

[Cybersecurity](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/cybersecurity/veilig/index.html)

06/10/2025 • 2 minutes reading

ABN AMRO Editorial

The NIS2 guideline places high demands on organizations and their suppliers. This European law obliges companies to improve their digital resilience. If a supplier does not meet cybersecurity requirements, your company can still be held responsible. It is therefore important to record clear agreements in the purchasing conditions.

With clear purchasing conditions you determine what you expect from suppliers. For example, about information security and risk management. This reduces the chance of misunderstandings and accelerates the preparation for NIS2. This way you prevent problems towards the deadline in 2026.

## Use sample clauses

Not every company has the time or expertise to write legal texts themselves. That is why Samen Digitaal Veilig offers [a free sample text](https://samendigitaalveilig.nl/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/20250207-Model-addendum-inkoop-supply-chain-NIS2.pdf) together with NEVI. You can include these in contracts, quotations or existing purchasing documents.

## Prepare the chain

Including NIS2 clauses in the purchasing conditions helps with legal compliance. You don't just strengthen your own organization. You also contribute to a safer digital ecosystem.
