# Volume growth is back in the food industry

[Podcast](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-spraak/podcast/index.html)

[Food](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/food/index.html)

08/14/2025 • 1 minute read

Melanie Murk

We expect the food industry to grow by 1 percent in volume by 2025. Domestic demand is increasing and exports are also increasing this year. We expect stabilization before 2026. The unpredictability, especially in international supply chains, is increasing. In this video, sector economist Melanie Murk explains in 2 minutes what challenges the food sector faces and what you can do as an entrepreneur.

## Security of supply and higher costs remain current

The extreme price increases are over for the time being, although some product groups such as coffee and cocoa are still affected as a result of crop failures due to climate change.

In addition, wage costs have risen sharply in recent years. In combination with a labor shortage, this leads to more investments in automation and robotization.
