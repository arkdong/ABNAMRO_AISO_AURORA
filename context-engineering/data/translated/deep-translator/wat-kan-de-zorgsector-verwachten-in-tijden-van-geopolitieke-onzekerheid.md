# What can the healthcare sector expect in times of geopolitical uncertainty?

[Podcast](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/in-spraak/podcast/index.html)

[Healthcare](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/sectoren-en-trends/healthcare/index.html)

08/14/2025 • 2 minutes reading

[Nehreen Al-Mahmoedi](https://www.abnamro.nl/nl/zakelijk/insights/authors/nehreen-al-mahmoedi/index.html)

The demand for care continues to increase because the number of vulnerable elderly people is increasing. Healthcare will therefore grow in 2025 and 2026. Innovation is necessary to meet the rising demand for healthcare. The trade situation with the United States hinders the investment climate necessary for healthcare innovation. In this video, sector analyst Nehreen Al-Mahmoedi explains in 2 minutes what consequences the trade war has for the healthcare sector and what you can do as an entrepreneur.

## Uncertainty inhibits the innovation that is needed

The growth expectations of the healthcare sector in 2025 and 2026 are explained in the video. Important developments such as staff shortages and increasing demand for care provide an explanation. In addition, the trade war waged by the United States and what this means for the healthcare sector are discussed. It is also important for healthcare institutions to be aware of these developments and to take appropriate steps.

## Developments in drug rates

This video was recorded for the trade deal between the United States and Europe. As part of the trade deal, the United States imposes a 15 percent tariff on medicines from Europe.
