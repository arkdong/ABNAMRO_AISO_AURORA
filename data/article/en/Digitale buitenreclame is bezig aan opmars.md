## Economic climate

The economic climate in the Netherlands is making a strong recovery from the coronavirus crisis and the expectation is that this upturn will continue in 2022. In the second quarter of 2021 both business confidence and the balance of entrepreneurs in the TMT sector who expect their revenue to rise are positive. However, consumer confidence fell for the second month in a row.

## Tight labour market

The labour market is currently very tight and the share of unfillable vacancies in the TMT sector is also rising. The share of unfillable vacancies for occupations related to the TMT sector has increased from 14 per cent in January of this year to almost 17 per cent at the end of July, according to ABN AMRO’s labour market indicator. Moreover, the staff shortage is occurring in all subsectors of the TMT sector.

## Digital outdoor advertising is on the rise

Media spending on digital outdoor advertising doubled in the second quarter of 2021. Due to the easing of coronavirus measures, consumers are once again out of the house and it pays for companies to advertise in shopping streets. In addition, the use of data offers a promising future for digital outdoor advertising.
