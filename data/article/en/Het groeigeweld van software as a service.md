Software-as-a-service (SaaS) is a relatively new business model for software companies, in which software is sold as an online subscription service. For several years now, ABN AMRO has successfully gained experience in financing SaaS companies.

What do the key developments and performance data (metrics) in the Dutch SaaS landscape look like? We found out for you through interviews and our own survey among Dutch SaaS entrepreneurs. We also spoke to venture capital firms that invest in SaaS companies.

Entrepreneurs cite managing international growth as one of the main challenges. Venture capital firms focus on global scalability and international growth. They set high requirements for a SaaS company’s metrics. What do those requirements look like? And what are the key metrics that Dutch entrepreneurs use to steer their company?

## In summary

- Software developers benefit from strong demand for SaaS
- Predictability makes SaaS a powerful business model
- International growth is a key challenge according to entrepreneurs
