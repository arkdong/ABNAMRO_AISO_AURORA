## Economic climate

The economic climate has become less favourable for the TMT sector. Exports are falling sharply and this in particular is causing a growth slowdown for the Dutch economy as a whole. Producer confidence is lower than before, but has stabilised at a still-high level. Other indicators such as consumer confidence and the purchasing managers’ index have clearly deteriorated.

## Staff shortage remains the biggest obstacle for the TMT sector

Business confidence in the TMT sector has fallen in recent quarters, but at 13.7 it is still high. No fewer than 52% of entrepreneurs say they experience no significant obstacles. The main obstacle remains the shortage of workers, which 31% are affected by.

## Spotlight: Advertising market

The online advertising market grew further last year and is taking an ever larger share of total net media spending. This is at the expense of print media, which are seeing their share of media spending shrink.
