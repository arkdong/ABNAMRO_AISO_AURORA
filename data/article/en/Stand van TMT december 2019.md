## Economic climate

The economic climate is deteriorating in the Netherlands. The purchasing managers’ index (PMI), producer confidence and consumer confidence have declined in recent quarters. Even so, entrepreneurs in the TMT sector are relatively positive compared with business as a whole.

## Staff shortage

What is affecting the TMT sector is a shortage of staff. No fewer than 33% of TMT entrepreneurs say they cannot obtain the required workforce. The shortage is particularly acute in IT. There, 41% of entrepreneurs struggle to find suitable staff.
