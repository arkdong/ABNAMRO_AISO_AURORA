## Economic climate

The economic climate is favourable for the TMT sector, although economic growth is slowing. Economic indicators are less positive than earlier this year, but still at high levels. Despite the slowdown, the signals are still green for the TMT sector.

## Few obstacles apart from staff shortages

Almost 55% of entrepreneurs in the sector say they do not face serious obstacles to growing their business. The obstacle that is often mentioned is the staff shortage. But at 29% this has fallen slightly compared with a quarter earlier.

## IT and TV producers are the strongest growers. Telecoms, publishers and printers remain in the red

IT is by far the largest segment within the TMT sector and also the fastest-growing. IT will continue to grow strongly in the coming years. The segment of TV producers is performing better than expected and will see turnover rise by 5% this year. On the other hand, telecoms, publishers and printers continue to shrink.
