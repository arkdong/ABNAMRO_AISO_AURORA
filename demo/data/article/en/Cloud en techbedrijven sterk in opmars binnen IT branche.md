# Cloud and tech companies on the rise in the IT sector

The IT sector is among the fastest-growing sectors in the Netherlands. It also plays a key role in driving digitalisation in other sectors. The report 'IT sector in focus' offers new insights by analysing company types, employment, growth expectations and the sector’s main activities. The report shows, among other things, that new types of IT business such as cloud and tech are growing rapidly.

The Dutch IT sector has over 55,000 companies in total. The largest segment consists of software companies (54 per cent), followed by system integrators and IT service providers (27 per cent), tech and internet companies (8 per cent), hosting and cloud providers (6 per cent), hardware companies (5 per cent) and data centres (0.5 per cent). Internet companies, hosting and cloud providers, and data centres are relatively new players and are growing rapidly. Due to the rise of cloud, 14 per cent of all companies already deal with these new forms of IT. The study shows that the majority of all IT professionals — 55 per cent — work for software companies. This segment also accounts for 41.9 per cent of total revenue in the IT market, followed by hardware suppliers (29.8 per cent) and system integrators and IT service providers (15.8 per cent). However, tech companies are the market segment where the most growth is expected in the coming years.

## IT sector already larger in size than the agricultural sector

The IT sector is expected to continue to grow in the coming years. Total revenue growth in 2018 was 7.4 per cent, and IT companies are positive about the future. Over half expect an increase or even a strong increase in revenue in the next three years. ABN AMRO expects growth of 5 per cent in 2019 and 4.5 per cent in 2020. “The IT sector has grown strongly over the past two decades. The sector is highly dynamic, with new emerging players introducing new revenue models. At present, over 200,000 people work in the IT sector, generating total revenue of € 90.3 billion.
